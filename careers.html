Now Hiring in Katy & League City, TX

Build Your Career at
Red River BBQ & Burger

Red River BBQ & Burger is a fast-growing Texas BBQ restaurant group with locations in Katy and League City. We're looking for passionate, hard-working people who take pride in great food and genuine hospitality. Whether you're starting your first job or looking to grow a long-term career in the restaurant industry, we want to hear from you.

Why Work at Red River BBQ & Burger?

We believe our team members are the heart of our restaurant. We invest in the people who make every plate and every guest experience count.

Competitive Pay

We offer competitive hourly wages and tips, with regular performance reviews and opportunities to grow your earnings over time.

Flexible Scheduling

We work with your availability — part-time, full-time, and weekend shifts available. Great for students, parents, and career changers.

Room to Grow

Many of our managers and kitchen leads started as entry-level team members. We promote from within and reward dedication with real career advancement.

Free Meals on Shift

Enjoy complimentary meals while you work. When you love what you serve, it shows — and we make sure you get to experience what makes Red River special.

Great Team Culture

We're a team-first kitchen. You'll work alongside people who have your back, managers who respect your time, and a culture built on Texas pride and good food.

Multiple Locations

With locations in Katy and League City, TX — and more on the way — find a location that works for where you live and where you want to grow.

Open Positions

We're currently hiring for the following roles at our Katy and League City, Texas locations. No experience? No problem — we'll train the right person.

Line Cook / Pit Cook

Full-Time & Part-Time · Katy & League City

Prepare and cook BBQ meats, sides, and burgers to Red River standards. Experience with smoked meats a plus but not required.

Server / Counter Staff

Full-Time & Part-Time · Katy & League City

Take orders, serve guests, and deliver the warm Texas hospitality we're known for. Prior restaurant experience preferred but not required.

To-Go / Cashier

Part-Time · Katy & League City

Handle online orders, curbside pickup, and in-person cashier duties. Strong communication skills and attention to detail are a must.

Dishwasher / Utility

Part-Time · Katy & League City

Keep the kitchen clean and organized. A great entry point to a restaurant career — many of our kitchen team started right here.
