Our Heritage

KATY FAVORITE SINCE 2011

Red River BBQ & Burger opened in Katy in 2011 to bring a family-friendly, Texas-style barbecue experience to the community. Enjoy a welcoming dining room, outdoor seating, and a kid-friendly play area—plus classic smoked meats, burgers, and homestyle sides made for everyday meals or weekend gatherings. We also offer catering for events of any size. View our catering menu for details, and we'll see you soon.

Josh Williams

General Manager
