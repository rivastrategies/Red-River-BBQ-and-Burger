Welcome to the Red River BBQ & Burger Party Room! Whether you're celebrating a birthday, hosting a corporate event, or gathering with friends and family, our private party room is the perfect venue for your special occasion. With authentic Texas BBQ and a warm, welcoming atmosphere, we make every event memorable.

Our party room can accommodate groups of various sizes and offers a comfortable, private space for your guests to enjoy. We provide flexible catering options to suit your needs, from our famous smoked meats to delicious sides and desserts. Let us handle the food while you focus on celebrating with your loved ones.

Ready to book your event? Contact us at 281.578.3800 or visit us at 1711 S Mason Rd, Katy, TX 77450 to discuss your party room reservation. We look forward to making your event a success!
