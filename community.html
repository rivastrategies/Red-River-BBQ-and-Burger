Red River Barbecue & Grill will donate 15% of sales generated by your participating group during your scheduled Spirit Night. To be eligible, guests must present a flyer or show phone confirmation to a team member when dining in or placing a takeout order. Only purchases made by guests who identify with the participating group will be included in the donation total. Donations are not based on total shift sales and exclude tax, gift card purchases, and third-party delivery services. Donation checks are issued within two weeks following the event.
